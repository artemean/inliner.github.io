Coffee

NDKW is a member of Strauss Group — one of the leading coffee company in the world. Being one of the biggest coffee company Strauss Group has collaborated for decades with the top coffee producers from Brazil, Colombia, Mexico, India and the Far East. All coffee procurement are organized via Strauss global procurement center in Switzerland. This network, in concert with an optimal production location (Hamburg) gives us possibilities to propose our clients always fresh, high quality coffee in the shortest period and at the top price.

For our clients, these resources translate to product solutions ranging from the traditional — regular and decaffeinated freeze dried coffee to specialized offerings such as certified coffees, rainforest and organics and customized new products, produced under the highest standards of quality control.
