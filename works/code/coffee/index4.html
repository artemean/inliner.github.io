About Company

Since 2011 Norddeutsche Kaffeewerke GmbH is a member of Strauss Coffee, one of the world’s leading coffee companies.

With the production startup in 2010 Norddeutsche Kaffeewerke is one of Europe’s most advanced and modern freeze-dried coffee factories.

Germany has always been famous for its advanced production technology and international quality standards.

The experience of NORDDEUTSCHE KAFFEEWERKE GMBH German specialists multiplied by the years of experience of Strauss technologists is not only a guaranty of high quality products, but it also optimizes the costs.



Geographic location of the factory is very convenient as for the European customer service, so as for shipments by sea — the factory location is close to Hamburg, which is an important cargo port.

The production capacity of the plant is 4500 tons of freeze dried coffee a year. The company employs more than 120 workers.
